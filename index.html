Team Calcide

About Us

We are "Calcide", a passionate team competing in the F1 in Schools challenge. United by our love for engineering and innovation, we work together to design, build, and race a miniature F1 car powered by compressed air.

Each member brings unique strengths—from design and engineering to marketing and management—helping us grow as a team and tackle every challenge with creativity and determination. Calcide is more than just a team name—it represents our drive to learn, collaborate, and push boundaries.

Our Goal

At Team Calcide, our goal for the F1 in Schools Nationals is to innovate, collaborate, and excel. We aim to design a high-performance miniature F1 car using engineering, creativity, and precision, while also developing strong branding, marketing, and team identity. Through dedication and teamwork, we strive not only to succeed on the track but also to inspire others with our passion for STEM and motorsport.
